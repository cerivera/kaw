Our Mission

Keep Austin Waggin was born through the understanding of how important it is to keep your pet happy and healthy. We will provide you peace of mind and your dog plenty of attention to suit his or her needs. Every dog is unique and we look forward to sitting down with you to discuss their personality.

FAQ

Where will you be walking my dog?
Your pet will be taken on a leashed walk within a one to two mile radius of your home.
What is your experience with different size dogs?
We have a variety of experience from tea cup to large breed but do not anticipate walking more than two large breeds at a time. (50 lbs. & up) This is for our safety and your pets'.
What vaccinations does my dog require?
Bordatella, Rabies & Distemper
Does my pet need to be spayed/neutered?
Yes.
Is Keep Austin Waggin' insured?
Yes. We are insured by Kennel Pro.

FAQ

What vaccinations does my dog require?
Bordatella, Rabies & Distemper
Does my pet need to be spayed/neutered?
Yes.
Will my pet be alone overnight?
Not when boarding at our location. Staff will be present from 9 pm until 7 am.
What should I bring for boarding?
We provide toys, bedding/kennel, complimentary snacks and dinner if appropriate. If your pet has special dietary needs we can discuss this at our meet n’ greet.
Will you administer medication?
We will administer pills in pill pocket form, anything else will need to be discussed prior to boarding.
Is potty trained required for boarding?
It is not a necessity but is highly suggested. Staff can discuss routines with you at Meet and Greet.
When will i meet my sitter?
After completion of pet survey, sitter will contact you to set up Meet and Greet.
