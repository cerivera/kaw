Our Mission

Keep Austin Waggin’ believes in just that; making sure our furry friends wag their tails without a worry in the world. Keep Austin Waggin’ was born through the understanding of how important it is to keep your pet happy and healthy. We will provide you peace of mind and your dog plenty of attention to suit his or her needs. Every dog is unique and we look forward to sitting down with you to discuss their personality. Belly rubs, treats & fetch are our expertise; let us take a load off your paws.
