FAQ

What vaccinations does my dog require?

Bordatella, Rabies & Distemper

Does my pet need to be spayed/neutered?

Yes.

Will my pet be alone overnight?

Not when boarding at our location. Staff will be present from 9 pm until 7 am.

What should I bring for boarding?

We provide toys, bedding/kennel, complimentary snacks and dinner if appropriate. If your pet has special dietary needs we can discuss this at our meet n’ greet.

Will you administer medication?

We will administer pills in pill pocket form, anything else will need to be discussed prior to boarding.

Is potty trained required for boarding?

It is not a necessity but is highly suggested. Staff can discuss routines with you at meet n’ greet.

What are pick up and drop off times for boarding?

Part of what we strive to offer is flexibility. We will do our best to meet your scheduling needs. Suggested time frames for drop off/pick up are:
6:30 am - 9:00 am
4:30 pm - 8:00 pm

When will i meet my sitter?

After completion of pet survey, sitter will contact you to set up meet n’ greet.
